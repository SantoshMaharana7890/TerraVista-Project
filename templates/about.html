Discover TerraVista

TerraVista is a unified tourism ecosystem designed to be your ultimate guide for exploring India's top cities. We bring together hidden nature spots, luxury and budget accommodations, and local transit routes into one seamless platform.

Curated Destinations

Explore the true essence of each city through historical monuments, cultural hubs, and breathtaking nature spots.

Smart Accommodations

Filter through premium hotels, budget lodges, and authentic local eateries to find exactly what fits your journey.

Live Transit Guides

Navigate like a local with safe transit routes, integrated maps, and direct access to local transportation.

Behind the Project

TerraVista was built from the ground up by final year B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering students. The goal of this major project was to eliminate the hassle of juggling multiple apps by providing a seamless, full-stack tourism portal powered by modern web technologies, dynamic API routing, and a robust cloud database.
